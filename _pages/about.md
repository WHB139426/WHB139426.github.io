I am currently a second-year master's student at CS Department, [Fudan University](https://cs.fudan.edu.cn/), where I am honored to be advised by Prof. [Weifeng Ge](https://www.weifengge.net/). I completed my B.E. degree at CS Department, [Southeast University](https://cse.seu.edu.cn/), where I am honored to work with Prof. [DING DING](https://dingdingseu.mystrikingly.com/). My research primarily focuses on **vision-language learning** (Visual Question Answering, Multimodal Large Langauge Models, etc.).
